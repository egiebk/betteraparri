# File a Complaint or Request Public Information

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Submit a complaint, request public information, or ask where to route a local government concern.

## Responsible Offices

Mayor's Office; Public Assistance and Complaints Desk; barangay offices; office concerned

## Requirements

- Name and contact information of requester
- Clear description of concern or information requested
- Location, date, office, or people involved, if applicable
- Supporting documents or photos, if available
- Authorization letter, if requesting records for another person

## Steps

1. Identify the concern and the office involved, if known.
2. Submit the complaint, request, or feedback to the public assistance desk or responsible office.
3. Provide supporting details and contact information.
4. Wait for acknowledgment, routing, or referral.
5. Follow up using the reference number or office instructions, if provided.

## Fees

Filing a complaint or feedback should not require a fee. Public information requests may have reproduction or certification fees, depending on the document.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on the nature of the concern, routing, and records requested.

## Location

Municipal Hall, barangay office, or office concerned in Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

For urgent safety, emergency, police, fire, or medical concerns, use official emergency channels instead of ordinary feedback routing.
