# Job Referrals and Skills Training

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Access job referrals, employment assistance, livelihood training, or skills training opportunities.

## Responsible Offices

Public Employment Service Office; Local Youth Development Office; Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office, depending on program

## Requirements

- Valid ID
- Resume or biodata for job referrals
- Proof of residency, if required
- Training application form, if applicable
- Supporting documents required by the employer or training provider

## Steps

1. Ask PESO or the responsible office about available job postings or training programs.
2. Submit your profile, resume, or application form.
3. Attend screening, orientation, or interview if required.
4. Follow referral, enrollment, or training instructions.
5. Keep contact information updated for notices.

## Fees

Confirm with the responsible office whether any training, certification, or document fee applies.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on employer timelines, training schedules, and program slots.

## Location

PESO or designated program office, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Program availability depends on partner agencies, employers, funding, and schedules.
