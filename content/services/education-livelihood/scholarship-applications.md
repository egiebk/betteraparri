# Scholarship Applications

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Apply for local scholarship or education assistance programs when available.

## Responsible Offices

Mayor's Office; Local Youth Development Office; Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office, depending on program

## Requirements

- Accomplished scholarship or education assistance form
- Valid school ID or proof of enrollment
- Certificate of registration or grades, if required
- Barangay certificate of residency
- Valid ID of applicant or parent or guardian
- Other documents required by the specific program

## Steps

1. Check whether scholarship or education assistance applications are open.
2. Secure the current program checklist.
3. Prepare and submit the required documents.
4. Wait for screening, validation, or interview.
5. Follow release or reporting instructions if approved.

## Fees

No application fee is typically expected. Confirm requirements with the responsible office.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on application period, screening, funding, and program rules.

## Location

Municipal Hall or designated program office, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Deadlines and eligibility requirements may change per scholarship cycle.
