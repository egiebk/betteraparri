# Emergency Assistance or Disaster Reporting

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Report emergencies, hazards, disaster impacts, or urgent assistance needs.

## Responsible Office

Municipal Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office

## Requirements

- Name and contact number of reporter
- Exact location of incident or affected household
- Type of emergency, hazard, or assistance needed
- Photos or supporting details, if safe to provide
- Barangay certification or damage report for post-disaster assistance, if required

## Steps

1. Contact the appropriate emergency hotline for urgent incidents.
2. Provide the location, type of incident, and immediate risks.
3. Follow response instructions from MDRRMO or emergency responders.
4. For post-disaster assistance, coordinate with the barangay and submit required documents.
5. Wait for validation, referral, or release instructions.

## Fees

No reporting fee is expected.

## Processing Time

Emergency response depends on incident severity, location, weather, and available responders. Assistance processing depends on validation and available resources.

## Location

MDRRMO or emergency response center, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

For immediate danger, call official emergency hotlines. Do not enter unsafe areas to gather photos or documents.
