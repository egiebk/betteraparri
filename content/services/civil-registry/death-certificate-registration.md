# Death Certificate Registration

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Register a death with the Local Civil Registrar and prepare the documents needed for burial, transfer, or records purposes.

## Responsible Office

Local Civil Registrar

## Requirements

- Death certificate or medical certificate of cause of death
- Valid ID of the informant or responsible person
- Barangay certification or supporting document, if required
- Burial or transfer permit documents, if applicable
- Other documents requested after review

## Steps

1. Secure the death certificate or medical certification.
2. Submit the documents to the Local Civil Registrar.
3. Review the encoded details before signing.
4. Pay any assessed fees, if applicable.
5. Claim the registered copy or related permit once released.

## Fees

Fees, if any, are based on applicable local ordinances.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on completeness of records and required verification.

## Location

Municipal Hall, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Ask the Local Civil Registrar about requirements for deaths that occurred outside Aparri, medico-legal cases, or late registration.
