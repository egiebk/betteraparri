# Late Birth Registration

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

File a delayed birth registration when a birth was not registered within the required period.

## Responsible Office

Local Civil Registrar

## Requirements

- Accomplished delayed birth registration form
- Negative certification or proof that no prior birth record exists, if required
- Baptismal certificate, school records, or other supporting records
- Valid IDs of the applicant, parent, or informant
- Affidavit or sworn statement explaining the delayed registration
- Other documents required after evaluation

## Steps

1. Ask the Local Civil Registrar to verify whether a birth record already exists.
2. Prepare the required forms, IDs, and supporting documents.
3. Submit the application for review.
4. Complete any publication, affidavit, or correction requirements if instructed.
5. Pay assessed fees and claim the registered record when released.

## Fees

Fees are based on applicable local ordinances and the type of documents required.

## Processing Time

Processing time may take longer than regular registration because supporting records must be verified.

## Location

Municipal Hall, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Delayed registration requirements depend on age, available records, and civil registry rules. Confirm the exact checklist with the Local Civil Registrar.
