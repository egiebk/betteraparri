# Marriage License Application

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Apply for a marriage license before a wedding ceremony.

## Responsible Office

Local Civil Registrar

## Requirements

- Marriage license application form
- Birth certificate or civil registry record of each applicant
- Valid IDs of both applicants
- Certificate of no marriage record, if required
- Parental consent or advice, if applicable by age
- Pre-marriage counseling certificate, if required
- Other supporting documents based on civil status

## Steps

1. Both applicants appear at the Local Civil Registrar.
2. Submit the application form and required documents.
3. Attend required counseling or orientation, if applicable.
4. Pay assessed fees.
5. Wait for the required posting or processing period.
6. Claim the marriage license once available.

## Fees

Fees are based on applicable local ordinances and document requirements.

## Processing Time

Processing time includes the required waiting or posting period. Confirm the current schedule with the Local Civil Registrar.

## Location

Municipal Hall, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Requirements vary for applicants who are widowed, annulled, foreign nationals, or below certain ages.
