# Birth Certificate Registration

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Register a child's birth with the Local Civil Registrar so an official birth record can be created.

## Responsible Office

Local Civil Registrar

## Requirements

- Accomplished birth registration form
- Certificate of live birth from the attending physician, midwife, or health facility
- Valid IDs of the parent or informant
- Marriage certificate of parents, if applicable
- Other supporting documents requested by the Local Civil Registrar

## Steps

1. Prepare the required documents.
2. Submit the documents to the Local Civil Registrar.
3. Review the encoded birth details before signing.
4. Pay any assessed fees, if applicable.
5. Claim the registered copy or follow the office instructions for release.

## Fees

Fees, if any, are based on applicable local ordinances and assessment by the responsible office.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on completeness of requirements and office verification.

## Location

Municipal Hall, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Requirements may vary depending on the applicant's situation. Please confirm with the Local Civil Registrar before visiting.
