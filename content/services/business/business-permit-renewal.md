# Business Permit Renewal

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Renew an existing business permit and settle required local business taxes and fees.

## Responsible Offices

Business Permits and Licensing Office; Municipal Treasurer's Office

## Requirements

- Previous business permit
- Barangay business clearance for the renewal year
- Accomplished renewal form
- Proof of gross receipts or sales, if required
- Fire safety inspection certificate or other clearances, if applicable
- Valid ID of owner or authorized representative

## Steps

1. Prepare the renewal documents.
2. Submit the renewal application to BPLO.
3. Proceed to assessment of business taxes and fees.
4. Pay assessed amounts at the Municipal Treasurer's Office.
5. Complete required inspections or clearances.
6. Claim the renewed permit.

## Fees

Fees and penalties are based on applicable local ordinances, renewal period, and assessment.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on completeness of requirements, payment, and inspections.

## Location

Municipal Hall, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Renew within the official renewal period to avoid penalties. Confirm current deadlines with BPLO or the Treasurer's Office.
