# New Business Permit

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Apply for a local permit to operate a business within Aparri.

## Responsible Office

Business Permits and Licensing Office

## Requirements

- Completed business permit application form
- Barangay business clearance
- DTI, SEC, or CDA registration, if applicable
- Lease contract or proof of ownership of business location
- Valid ID of owner or authorized representative
- Fire safety inspection certificate, if required
- Other clearances based on business type

## Steps

1. Secure barangay business clearance.
2. Submit the application and requirements to the BPLO.
3. Wait for assessment of local taxes, fees, and required clearances.
4. Pay assessed fees at the Municipal Treasurer's Office.
5. Complete required inspections or clearances.
6. Claim the business permit when approved.

## Fees

Fees are based on business type, capitalization, activity, and applicable local revenue rules.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on completeness of requirements and required inspections.

## Location

Municipal Hall, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Requirements may vary by business activity. Please confirm the current checklist with BPLO before visiting.
