# Building Permit

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Apply for a building permit before starting construction, renovation, or other covered works.

## Responsible Offices

Municipal Engineering Office; Zoning or Planning Office

## Requirements

- Accomplished building permit application forms
- Proof of ownership or authority to build
- Building plans and technical documents signed by licensed professionals, if required
- Barangay clearance or locational clearance, if required
- Fire safety evaluation documents, if applicable
- Other clearances based on project type and location

## Steps

1. Ask the Engineering Office for the current checklist.
2. Secure required plans, ownership documents, and clearances.
3. Submit the application for technical review.
4. Pay assessed fees.
5. Complete corrections or inspections if required.
6. Claim the permit before beginning construction.

## Fees

Fees are based on project type, floor area, valuation, and applicable local or national rules.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on completeness of plans, required clearances, and technical review.

## Location

Municipal Hall or designated Engineering Office service area, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Do not start covered construction work without confirming permit requirements with the Engineering Office.
