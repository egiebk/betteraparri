# Farmer or Fisherfolk Registration

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Register as a farmer or fisherfolk for local and national agriculture or fishery programs.

## Responsible Offices

Municipal Agriculture Office; fisheries or BFAR-linked program desk, where applicable

## Requirements

- Valid ID
- Barangay certificate of residency or certification of farming or fishing activity
- Farm, boat, gear, or livelihood details, if applicable
- Existing registry number or record, if available
- Other documents required by the program

## Steps

1. Visit the Municipal Agriculture Office or designated registration desk.
2. Submit identity, residency, and activity documents.
3. Complete the registration form.
4. Wait for validation or encoding.
5. Keep the registration record for future program applications.

## Fees

Confirm with the responsible office whether any fee applies.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on validation, registry system availability, and completeness of documents.

## Location

Municipal Agriculture Office or designated service area, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Some assistance programs require registration before application or release of benefits.
