# Agricultural or Fishery Assistance

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Request available seed, fertilizer, fingerling, equipment, livelihood, or disaster-related assistance.

## Responsible Offices

Municipal Agriculture Office; fisheries or BFAR-linked program desk, where applicable

## Requirements

- Valid ID
- Proof of registration as farmer, fisherfolk, association member, or beneficiary, if required
- Barangay certification or endorsement, if required
- Farm, fishery, boat, gear, or damage details depending on request
- Program-specific application form

## Steps

1. Ask the Municipal Agriculture Office about available programs and schedules.
2. Prepare the program-specific requirements.
3. Submit the application or report for validation.
4. Wait for assessment, prioritization, or approval.
5. Follow release, training, or monitoring instructions.

## Fees

Assistance programs are subject to program rules. Confirm if any counterpart, membership, or documentation cost applies.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on program availability, validation, funding, and supply schedules.

## Location

Municipal Agriculture Office or designated program site, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

For typhoon, flood, crop, livestock, or fishery damage, report as soon as possible after the incident.
