# RHU Consultation

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Visit the Rural Health Unit for basic consultation and primary health services.

## Responsible Office

Municipal Health Office / Rural Health Unit

## Requirements

- Valid ID or patient information
- Barangay health record, if available
- Previous prescription, laboratory result, or referral, if relevant
- Parent or guardian for minors

## Steps

1. Go to the RHU during service hours.
2. Register at the receiving or records area.
3. Wait for triage, consultation, or referral.
4. Follow the health worker's instructions for medicine, laboratory tests, or follow-up.

## Fees

Basic public health services may be free, but confirm any charges for certificates, tests, or special services.

## Processing Time

Waiting time depends on patient volume and the type of service needed.

## Location

Rural Health Unit or designated municipal health facility, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Schedules may change during outreach activities, emergencies, or holidays. Confirm availability before visiting.
