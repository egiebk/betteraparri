## Steps

1. Secure a doctor's request and bring your patient record.
2. Confirm the schedule for the test you need.
3. Present your PhilHealth number, if available.
4. Register at the laboratory and follow instructions for specimen collection or testing.
5. Pay the assessed fee, if applicable.
6. Return or wait for the release of results based on laboratory instructions.

## Fees

Fees are based on Ordinance No. 2024-107. Some tests may be free with a PhilHealth number or under a DOH program, as shown in the price list.

## Processing Time

Processing and release time depends on the test, patient volume, specimen requirements, and laboratory availability.

## Location

Aparri Municipal Health Center - East Clinical Laboratory

## Notes

- Please present your doctor's request, record, and PhilHealth number.
- Please inquire with the laboratory for availability and schedule.
- Schedules may change during holidays, emergencies, outreach activities, or staff availability.
