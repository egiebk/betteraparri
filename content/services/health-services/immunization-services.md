# Immunization Services

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Access routine immunization services for eligible children, pregnant women, and other priority groups.

## Responsible Office

Municipal Health Office / Rural Health Unit

## Requirements

- Child health card or immunization record, if available
- Parent or guardian information
- Valid ID of parent or guardian, if required
- Pregnancy record or health card, if applicable

## Steps

1. Confirm the current immunization schedule with the RHU or barangay health worker.
2. Bring the health card and required information.
3. Register and wait for screening.
4. Receive the vaccine if eligible.
5. Update the immunization record and follow the next schedule.

## Fees

Routine public immunization services are generally provided through public health programs. Confirm any special requirements with the RHU.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on schedule, screening, and patient volume.

## Location

RHU, barangay health station, or designated outreach site in Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Eligibility and vaccine availability depend on Department of Health and local program schedules.
