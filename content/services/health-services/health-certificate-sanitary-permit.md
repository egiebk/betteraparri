# Health Certificate or Sanitary Permit

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Request a health certificate or sanitary permit for work, business, or regulatory requirements.

## Responsible Office

Municipal Health Office / Sanitation Office

## Requirements

- Accomplished application form
- Valid ID
- Required laboratory or medical results, if applicable
- Business permit or establishment details for sanitary permit requests
- Proof of payment, if required

## Steps

1. Ask the Municipal Health Office for the current checklist.
2. Complete required medical, laboratory, or inspection steps.
3. Submit documents for review.
4. Pay assessed fees, if applicable.
5. Claim the certificate or permit once approved.

## Fees

Fees are based on applicable local ordinances and the type of certificate or permit requested.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on completeness of requirements, test results, and inspections.

## Location

Municipal Health Office or designated sanitation service area, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Requirements may differ for food handlers, establishments, and other regulated activities.
