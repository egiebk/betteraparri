# Access Maternal Care & Child Immunization

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Access maternal care, pregnancy-related health services, and child immunization guidance through the Municipal Health Office / Rural Health Unit.

## Responsible Office

Municipal Health Office / Rural Health Unit

## Requirements

- Valid ID or patient information
- Pregnancy record, maternal health card, or child health card, if available
- Previous prescription, laboratory result, or referral, if relevant
- Parent or guardian information for child immunization
- PhilHealth number, if available

## Pregnancy-Related Laboratory Schedule

Aparri Municipal Health Center - East Clinical Laboratory has posted serological testing for pregnant patients on:

| Day    | Schedule  |
| ------ | --------- |
| Monday | Afternoon |
| Friday | Afternoon |

Other laboratory services may be scheduled on different days. See [Clinical Laboratory Services](/services/health-services/clinical-laboratory-services) for the full posted laboratory schedule and price list.

## Steps

1. Confirm the current schedule with the RHU, barangay health worker, or laboratory.
2. Bring the required health card, record, or doctor's request.
3. Register and wait for screening, consultation, immunization, or laboratory instructions.
4. Follow the next visit, laboratory, or immunization schedule given by health staff.

## Fees

Routine public health services may be free through public health programs. Laboratory fees depend on the requested test, residency, PhilHealth status, and applicable ordinance.

## Processing Time

Waiting time depends on schedule, screening, patient volume, and test availability.

## Location

RHU, barangay health station, designated outreach site, or Aparri Municipal Health Center - East Clinical Laboratory

## Notes

Pregnancy-related testing, immunization, and maternal care schedules may change depending on vaccine supply, laboratory availability, holidays, emergencies, or outreach activities.
