# PWD ID

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Apply for a person with disability identification card for eligible residents.

## Responsible Office

Persons with Disability Affairs Office or Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office

## Requirements

- Accomplished PWD ID application form
- Valid ID or proof of identity
- Barangay certificate of residency, if required
- Medical certificate or disability assessment document
- Recent ID photo, if required

## Steps

1. Prepare proof of identity, residency, and disability.
2. Submit the application to the responsible office.
3. Complete verification or assessment.
4. Wait for approval and ID processing.
5. Claim the PWD ID or follow release instructions.

## Fees

Confirm with the responsible office whether any replacement or processing fee applies.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on verification and ID availability.

## Location

Municipal Hall or designated social welfare service area, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Eligibility and required medical documents may vary depending on disability category.
