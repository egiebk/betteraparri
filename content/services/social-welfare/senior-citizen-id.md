# Senior Citizen ID

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Apply for or renew a senior citizen identification card for eligible residents.

## Responsible Office

Office for Senior Citizens Affairs

## Requirements

- Valid ID or birth record showing date of birth
- Recent ID photo, if required
- Barangay certificate of residency, if required
- Accomplished application form

## Steps

1. Prepare proof of age and residency.
2. Submit the application to OSCA or the designated office.
3. Wait for verification and encoding.
4. Claim the senior citizen ID or follow release instructions.

## Fees

Confirm with OSCA whether any replacement or processing fee applies.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on verification and ID availability.

## Location

Municipal Hall or designated OSCA service area, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Applicants must meet the eligibility requirements under applicable senior citizen rules.
