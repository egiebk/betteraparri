# Burial Assistance

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Request burial or funeral assistance for eligible families.

## Responsible Office

Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office

## Requirements

- Valid ID of requester
- Death certificate or proof of death
- Funeral contract, billing statement, or related expense document
- Barangay certificate of residency or indigency, if required
- Proof of relationship to the deceased

## Steps

1. Visit the MSWDO for initial assessment.
2. Submit documents for review.
3. Complete the required interview or social case assessment.
4. Wait for approval or referral.
5. Follow release instructions from the office.

## Fees

No application fee is typically expected. Assistance depends on eligibility and available funds.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on assessment, document completeness, and fund availability.

## Location

Municipal Hall or designated MSWDO service area, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Please confirm current requirements with MSWDO before visiting.
