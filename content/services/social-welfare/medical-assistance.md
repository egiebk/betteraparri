# Medical Assistance

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Request medical assistance for eligible residents who need support for treatment, medicines, hospital bills, or related expenses.

## Responsible Office

Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office

## Requirements

- Valid ID of the patient or requester
- Medical certificate, prescription, hospital bill, or treatment plan
- Barangay certificate of residency or indigency, if required
- Proof of relationship if requesting for a family member
- Other documents required after assessment

## Steps

1. Visit the MSWDO for assessment.
2. Submit the required documents.
3. Complete the interview or social case assessment.
4. Wait for approval, referral, or assistance instructions.
5. Claim assistance or follow the release process provided by the office.

## Fees

No application fee is typically expected, but assistance depends on eligibility and available funds.

## Processing Time

Processing time depends on assessment, document completeness, and fund availability.

## Location

Municipal Hall or designated MSWDO service area, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Assistance is subject to eligibility, program rules, and available resources.
