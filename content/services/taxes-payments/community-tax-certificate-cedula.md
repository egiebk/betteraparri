# Community Tax Certificate or Cedula

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Request a community tax certificate for personal, employment, business, or transaction requirements.

## Responsible Office

Municipal Treasurer's Office

## Requirements

- Valid ID
- Basic personal information
- Income or business information, if applicable
- Authorization letter and representative ID, if applying through a representative

## Steps

1. Go to the Municipal Treasurer's Office.
2. Provide the required information and ID.
3. Wait for computation of the community tax amount.
4. Pay the assessed amount.
5. Review and claim the community tax certificate.

## Fees

The amount is based on applicable rules and the applicant's declared income, business, or property information.

## Processing Time

Usually processed within the same visit when requirements are complete.

## Location

Municipal Hall, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

Please confirm the current basis for computation with the Municipal Treasurer's Office.
