# Real Property Tax Payment

> We are currently working with the LGU to review and finalize this information to ensure its accuracy.

Pay real property tax for land, building, or other taxable real property.

## Responsible Office

Municipal Treasurer's Office

## Requirements

- Latest real property tax receipt, if available
- Tax declaration number or property identification details
- Valid ID of owner or payer
- Authorization letter, if paying on behalf of another person

## Steps

1. Present the latest tax receipt or property details.
2. Request assessment of current tax, discounts, or penalties.
3. Review the assessed amount.
4. Pay at the Municipal Treasurer's Office.
5. Claim the official receipt.

## Fees

Amount due depends on assessed property value, payment period, discounts, and penalties.

## Processing Time

Usually processed within the same visit when property records are available.

## Location

Municipal Hall, Aparri, Cagayan

## Notes

For updated assessments or missing records, the Treasurer's Office may coordinate with the Assessor's Office.
