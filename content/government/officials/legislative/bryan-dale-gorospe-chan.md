## About Vice Mayor Bryan Dale Gorospe Chan

Vice Mayor Bryan Dale Gorospe Chan serves as the presiding officer of the Sangguniang Bayan of Aparri, Cagayan for the 2025-2028 term.

As Municipal Vice Mayor, Vice Mayor Chan helps ensure that legislative sessions run properly and that ordinances and resolutions support the development goals of Aparri.
