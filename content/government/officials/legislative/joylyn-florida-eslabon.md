## About Councilor Joylyn Florida Eslabon

Councilor Joylyn Florida Eslabon is a dedicated member of the Sangguniang Bayan of Aparri, Cagayan, serving the community for the 2025-2028 term.

As a Sanguniang Bayan Member, Councilor Eslabon works on legislation, ordinances, and resolutions that affect the daily lives of Aparri residents. Her focus includes community development, public services, and local governance initiatives.
