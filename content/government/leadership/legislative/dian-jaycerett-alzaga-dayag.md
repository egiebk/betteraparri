## About Councilor Dian Jaycerett Alzaga Dayag

Councilor Dian Jaycerett Alzaga Dayag is a dedicated member of the Sangguniang Bayan of Aparri, Cagayan, serving the community for the 2025-2028 term.

As a Sanguniang Bayan Member, Councilor Dayag works on legislation, ordinances, and resolutions that affect the daily lives of Aparri residents. Her focus includes community development, public services, and local governance initiatives.
