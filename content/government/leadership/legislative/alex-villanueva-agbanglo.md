# Alex Villanueva Agbanglo

## Sanguniang Bayan Member

## About Councilor Alex Villanueva Agbanglo

Councilor Alex Villanueva Agbanglo is a dedicated member of the Sangguniang Bayan of Aparri, Cagayan, serving the community for the 2025-2028 term.

As a Sanguniang Bayan Member, Councilor Agbanglo works on legislation, ordinances, and resolutions that affect the daily lives of Aparri residents. His focus includes community development, public services, and local governance initiatives.
